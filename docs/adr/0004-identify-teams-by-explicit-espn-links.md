# Identify Teams by explicit ESPN links

The ESPN ingestion boundary will identify Teams from the numeric ESPN Team ID in their explicit team links, not from display names or emblem URLs. A previously unseen valid ESPN Team ID creates the Team and its Team Emblem automatically; malformed or ID-less rows are logged for review. Existing Matches will be migrated in a one-time backfill that creates Teams from their distinct legacy names, downloads their retained emblems, and links the Matches; unresolved records are reported rather than discarded. The ESPN Team ID is optional until both results and calendar markup are verified; rows without it use the name-and-alias fallback. This avoids treating mutable presentation values or CDN asset paths as identity while allowing the scraper to keep importing valid data during the transition.
